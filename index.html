AnyStatus

A remote control for your CI/CD pipelines and more

AnyStatus is an open-source desktop application that brings together Metrics and Events from various sources into one simple dashboard. AnyStatus integrates with Azure DevOps, Jenkins, Docker, Binance, and more. You can also develop your own custom plugins and widgets.
